Our Services

The care and support for your business is the biggest benefit that you will receive as a valued Thacker customer. Thacker is eager to assist your funeral home in developing creative solutions to the business issues that funeral homes face in today's changing business environment.

iArrange by Thacker

The industry's most comprehensive interactive virtual showroom designed exclusively to help you show and sell caskets is here. Our innovative, intuitive interface makes it easier for you to give families the help and advice they need to make final arrangements for their loved ones.

Remembrance Line

The Remembrance Line of caskets is designed to offer you and your family the advantages of both a cremation service and a traditional burial. They use a removable container that is used for the cremation process, offering a personal touch while still offering the value of cremation.

Merchandising Solutions

Whether your funeral home uses a traditional full size room, a modular casket display, or an intuitive online environment, Thacker can develop a successful merchandising program that will suit the preferences of the families you serve.

Personalization Services

Families have shown a great interest in being able to make the funeral more personal to the life of the deceased. Thacker provides a number of ways to incorporate "Personalization" into the casket as well as the funeral.

Training & Support

Education should be the foundation of any casket presentation to client families. Thacker is here to help develop training specifically geared to the needs of your funeral home and the follow up support to make it successful.

On Demand Service

Thacker Caskets, Inc. is here to service your funeral home at any time. We pride ourselves in providing our customers the best delivery service in the industry. No matter what time you need us we are here for you.

Cremation

Families selecting cremation often have a wide variety of preferences with regards to not only the type of casket, but the type of service. Thacker Caskets offers a wide range of cremation products to meet these varied needs.
