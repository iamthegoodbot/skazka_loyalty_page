About Thacker

Learn about our company and our dedication to our customers, our employees, and the entire funeral industry...

Our History

Thacker Caskets has a rich history from being founded in 1939 in Washington D.C., to now being the largest family owned casket company in the United States. Our heritage is truly an American success story.

Our Team

Thacker Caskets employs over 250 American workers, all dedicated to serving your funeral home with the utmost level of customer service, all respecting the sacred duty the casket serves.

Our Locations

Thacker Caskets services a total of 33 states between its distribution and manufacturing operations. Thacker Caskets, Inc., the distribution business, has 13 warehouses that service a total of 16 states.

Customer Testimonials

Thacker Caskets keeps its core competencies at heart and by doing this, we have not only strengthened our business, but the business of our loyal funeral home customers.

Modern Marvels Feature

Thacker Caskets had the proud honor of being featured on the History Channel's longtime series, Modern Marvels. Thacker Casket Manufacturing and its production processes received generous airtime!

Careers

Thacker Caskets is constantly seeking qualified professionals to join our growing team. We welcome intelligent individuals with creativity and a customer oriented attitude to apply to Thacker for any type of position.

Affiliations

At Thacker Caskets, we believe it to be important to network with our peers so that we can make adequate business decisions based on the latest of what is happening in today's market. Thus, Thacker has affiliations with a wide range of industry organizations.
