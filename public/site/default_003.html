The Thacker Difference

The "Thacker Difference" is composed of the differentiating factors that set our company and our product apart from other casket manufacturers and suppliers within the funeral industry. Each of these factors have been integral to the success and growth of our company over the years.

Family Owned & Operated

With a grassroots start in the heart of Washington, D.C. in 1939, the company built its foundation on providing an honest product at an honest price.

Innovative Product

Thacker Caskets, Inc. stays in tune to what funeral homes need in a valued supplier in today's volatile business market, combining eye appeal and build quality to produce a finely made casket.

Made In America

All of Thacker Caskets metal caskets are made right here in the United States by American workers at our own manufacturing plant in Florence, Alabama

Committed Customer Service

Committed customer service has been a cornerstone of our business since its inception in 1939. Our team is here to provide you answers when you need them.

Environmental Responsibility

Thacker Caskets understands the importance and necessity of being an environmentally responsible company in today’s business landscape – something our business strives toward every day.
